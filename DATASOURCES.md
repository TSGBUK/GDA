# Data Sources

## Gridwatch Data

Gridwatch data is downloaded from the [gridwatch.templar.co](https://gridwatch.templar.co) website.
If you download your own copy, please donate.

## NESO Data

Balancing, Demand, Frequency, Historical Generation, and Inertia data are all from [NESO](https://neso.energy).
Our hashes match theirs.

## Weather Data

Weather data is from the Open Weather API.
If you use it, make sure you save your requests: vast amounts of data are needed, so save what you get.
Please do not abuse free services.

## National Grid    

National Grid and Voltage data are from the national grids open portal.

Again please dont abuse a free service.
