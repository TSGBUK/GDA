About GDA and the Author

GDA (Grid Data Analysis) is a long-horizon engineering effort to consolidate UK grid operations data into clean, reusable, and inference-friendly datasets. It focuses on deterministic pipelines, explicit schemas, and practical tooling that can run reliably in home and demo environments.

Mark S (TSGB UK) brings a 20-year HPC background to the project, emphasizing throughput, reproducibility, and observability across data ingestion, parquet conversion, and downstream analytics. The current implementation spans replay applications, snapshot/dashboard planning, and machine-learning parquet foundations.
