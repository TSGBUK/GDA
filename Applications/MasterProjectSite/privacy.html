Privacy Policy

GDA (Grid Data Analysis) values privacy and aims to keep this site lightweight, transparent, and limited in data collection. This policy explains what is collected, how it is used, and how to request changes.

Data We Collect

The site is primarily informational. Data collection is limited to:

  • Contact Inputs: Name, email, and message content submitted by you through the contact form.
  • Basic Access Data: Standard web request metadata required to deliver pages and maintain service reliability.
  • Optional Cookies: Minimal browser storage used for site behavior where enabled by your environment.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used only for operational and communication needs:

  1. Respond to collaboration or support enquiries related to GDA.
  2. Maintain site stability and troubleshoot reliability issues.
  3. Improve documentation quality and roadmap communication.

Third-Party Data and External Sources

GDA processes public and licensed grid datasets in backend workflows. This privacy policy covers this website and contact process only; source dataset licensing remains governed by each original provider.

Retention and Deletion

Contact messages are retained only as long as needed to respond and track active work conversations.

  • Inactive contact records are periodically removed.
  • You can request correction or deletion of your contact-submitted details.
  • Operational logs may be retained briefly for security and debugging.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of submitted personal contact information by emailing mark.s@tsgb.uk.
